Compression Research
Designing and evaluating compression algorithms to reduce the size of microscopy images for digital pathology.
Whole Slide Image Compression Using JPEG XL
Production pipeline for compressing Whole Slide Images (WSI) using JPEG XL with quality-constrained optimization. Implements adaptive tissue masking, intelligent tile selection, and parallel processing to achieve visually lossless compression (SSIM ≥ 0.995) with 12:1 compression ratios for histopathology images.
Comparative Evaluation of Image Compression Codecs for Whole-Slide Pathology Images
A systematic benchmark comparing compression codecs for whole-slide imaging in digital pathology. The study evaluates JPEG, JPEG-XL, and JPEG-2000 using SSIM-matched quality assessment across 1,000 tissue tiles, revealing JPEG-XL's superior compression efficiency. The repository contains the full experimental pipeline, including mask-guided tile sampling, bisection-based quality matching, and comprehensive statistical analysis.
